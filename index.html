About Me

I'm a GIS Technician II for Guadalupe County, TX, focusing on address management, street naming, land records, and public safety datasets. My work centers on automating repetitive tasks and building reliable GIS workflows that reduce errors and speed up operations.

I design ArcGIS Pro Tasks, Python toolboxes, and Excel-integrated systems that streamline everyday workflows such as address verification, data entry, and street name conflict checks.

I graduated from Texas State University in 2024 with a B.S. in Geographic Information Science and a minor in Nature and Heritage Tourism, which grounded me in both the technical side of GIS and how people experience and interact with place.

Projects

A selection of real-world work where I improved GIS operations, automated workflows, or designed better spatial datasets for county and public safety use.

Address Verification Workflow Automation

ArcGIS Pro · Python Toolbox · Excel Integration

Developed a complete address verification workflow that selects parcels, checks address history, populates an Excel workbook, and generates address verification letters in ArcGIS Pro.

Impact: Reduced manual steps, eliminated common data-entry errors, and standardized the entire address assignment process.

Street Name Conflict & Phonetic Matching Tool

ArcPy · Metaphone · Fuzzy Matching

Created a street-name conflict detection tool combining substring matching, Metaphone pronunciation encoding, and fuzzy string scoring to catch sound-alike names across centerlines and reserved-name datasets.

Impact: Reduced risk of confusing street names, improved 911 routing clarity, and increased review consistency.

Data Integrity Framework for Address Points

Domains · Attribute Rules · Topology

Designed a comprehensive set of data integrity rules, domains, and topology checks for address points and street centerlines, ensuring accuracy across the address database.

Impact: Shifted QA from reactive to proactive, preventing invalid inputs at the source.
